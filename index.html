About Me


Hi, I'm Bertan, a Master's student in Computer Science at the University of Bonn. My research interests include 3D Computer Vision, Deep Learning, and Intelligent Systems. Currently, I am preparing my Master's thesis.

I work as a research assistant at the Autonomous Intelligent Systems Group, supervised by Prof. Dr. Sven Behnke. My work spans Person Detection, Re-Identification and Tracking using multiple sensors, Robotics Systems Integration, and 3D Scene Understanding. I develop and implement methods for our domestic service robots participating in the RoboCup@Home competitions.

Outside of my work and studies, I enjoy playing the piano, reading science-fiction, and running.
